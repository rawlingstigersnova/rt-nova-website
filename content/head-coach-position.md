---
title: "Head Coach Position"
---

Head Coaches lead team culture, player development, practice planning, game management, and family communication.

## Responsibilities

- Plan and lead practices
- Set expectations for players and families
- Manage games and tournament preparation
- Communicate consistently with families
- Support program standards and player development goals

## Qualifications

- Baseball coaching or playing experience
- Strong communication and organization
- Commitment to player development
- Alignment with Rawlings Tigers NOVA culture
