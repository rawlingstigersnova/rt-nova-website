---
title: "Assistant Coach Position"
---

Assistant Coaches support player development, practice execution, game preparation, and team communication.

## Responsibilities

- Assist with practice planning and station work
- Support player instruction and development
- Help with game-day preparation and dugout organization
- Reinforce team standards and culture

## Qualifications

- Baseball coaching or playing experience preferred
- Strong communication and reliability
- Development-first mindset
- Willingness to support the head coach and program standards
